# Code of conduct

Be respectful, technical, and evidence-led. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, spam, fabricated evidence, credential sharing, and manipulation of repository engagement are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove content that violates these standards and may restrict participation when necessary to keep the projects safe and useful.

To report conduct that should not be discussed publicly, request a private contact channel through a minimal issue without including sensitive personal information.
